Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2025

Snark Attack ("the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that observes your Google search queries and uses them to generate humorous, AI-powered commentary. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

What Data Is Collected

The extension collects Google search queries — specifically, the text you enter into Google Search. No other browsing activity, page content, or personal information is collected.

How Data Is Stored

Your search history, roast history, access code, and preferences (such as roast tone and frequency) are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage. This data does not leave your device except as described below.

How Data Is Used

Recent search queries are sent to the extension's backend server for the sole purpose of generating a roast response via Google's Gemini AI. Specifically:

  • A short list of your recent search queries is included in the request to the AI model.
  • An access code is included to verify you are an authorized user.
  • No other information is transmitted.

Search queries are not stored, sold, or used for any purpose beyond generating roast responses.

Third-Party Services

The extension relies on two third-party services to function:

  • Cloudflare Workers — the extension's backend proxy runs on Cloudflare's infrastructure. Cloudflare may retain standard server logs (such as IP addresses and request metadata) in accordance with Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini AI — search queries are forwarded to Google's Gemini API to generate responses, subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

Your data is never sold to third parties, used for advertising, or shared for any purpose unrelated to the extension's core function of generating roast responses.

Data Retention

Search history and roast history stored locally are capped at a rolling limit and can be cleared at any time by removing the extension or clearing Chrome's extension storage. The extension does not retain your data on any server.

Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the "Last updated" date above will be revised. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
